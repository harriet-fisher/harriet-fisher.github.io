Consulate of Colombia AI Chatbot

Tools: Python, Twillio, Tensorflow

Role: Software Developer, Operations Strategist

In response to the challenges faced by the Colombian Consulate, including high call volumes, repetitive questions, and frequent errors in appointment bookings and document preparations, we developed an AI-powered chatbot. This chatbot aims to provide immediate, 24/7 assistance to users, guiding them through the appointment booking process, answering common queries, and ensuring that all necessary documents are correctly prepared prior to appointments. The bot is accessible via WhatsApp, catering to users who find complex web layouts challenging or have limited access to desktop devices, thus ensuring wider accessibility and ease of use. Features include:

  • Natural Language Processing: Utilizes advanced NLP techniques to understand and respond to user inquiries in natural language, enabling a seamless and intuitive user experience.
  • Dynamic FAQ: Dynamically generates responses to frequently asked questions, reducing the need for human intervention and ensuring consistency in the information provided.
  • Document Checklist: Provides users with a customized list of required documents based on the purpose of their appointment, ensuring that all necessary preparations are made in advance.

Polaris: Safety App

Tools: Ruby, Java, Adobe CC

Role: Software Developer, Creative Designer

Polaris is a crowd-sourced crime tracking app designed to enhance personal safety and community awareness. Developed using Ruby and Java, Polaris provides real-time crime data and safety features to help users navigate their environments more securely. Key features of the app include:

  • Path Optimization: Leverage real-time crime data to suggest the safest routes, helping users avoid areas with ongoing or recent criminal activity.
  • Crime Filtering: Users can filter crime reports by type, such as theft, assault, or vandalism, to get detailed information relevant to their concerns.
  • User Reporting: Empower the community by allowing users to report crimes and suspicious activities directly through the app, contributing to a comprehensive crime database.
  • Emergency Contact Alerts: In case of emergencies, users can send alerts to their pre-set emergency contacts, including their current location information, ensuring timely assistance.
