CoverUp: AI Cover Letter Writer

Tools: Java, HTML/CSS, OpenAI API

Role: Software Developer, Creative Designer

CoverUp is an intelligent AI-powered application designed to streamline the job application process by generating personalized cover letters. Utilizing advanced natural language processing, CoverUp crafts high-quality cover letters based on the user’s resume, job description, company details, and any specific skills the user wishes to highlight. Key features of the app include:

  • Resume Integration: Seamlessly import resume details to ensure your cover letter accurately reflects your professional experience and qualifications.
  • Job Description Analysis: Tailor each cover letter to the specific job by analyzing the job description, ensuring your application stands out to potential employers.
  • Company Insights: Incorporate relevant company information to demonstrate your interest and knowledge about the organization.
  • Highlight Special Skills: Emphasize unique skills and achievements that set you apart from other candidates.
  • Cover Letter Storage: Store and access past cover letters for up to 60 days, allowing for easy retrieval and reuse for similar job applications.

Polaris: Safety App

Tools: Ruby, Java, Adobe CC

Role: Software Developer, Creative Designer

Polaris is a crowd-sourced crime tracking app designed to enhance personal safety and community awareness. Developed using Ruby and Java, Polaris provides real-time crime data and safety features to help users navigate their environments more securely. Key features of the app include:

  • Path Optimization: Leverage real-time crime data to suggest the safest routes, helping users avoid areas with ongoing or recent criminal activity.
  • Crime Filtering: Users can filter crime reports by type, such as theft, assault, or vandalism, to get detailed information relevant to their concerns.
  • User Reporting: Empower the community by allowing users to report crimes and suspicious activities directly through the app, contributing to a comprehensive crime database.
  • Emergency Contact Alerts: In case of emergencies, users can send alerts to their pre-set emergency contacts, including their current location information, ensuring timely assistance.
