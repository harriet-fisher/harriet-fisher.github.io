Consulate of Colombia AI Chatbot

Tools: Python, Twillio, Tensorflow

Role: Software Developer, Operations Strategist

In response to the challenges faced by the Colombian Consulate, including high call volumes, repetitive questions, and frequent errors in appointment bookings and document preparations, we developed an AI-powered chatbot. This chatbot aims to provide immediate, 24/7 assistance to users, guiding them through the appointment booking process, answering common queries, and ensuring that all necessary documents are correctly prepared prior to appointments. The bot is accessible via WhatsApp, catering to users who find complex web layouts challenging or have limited access to desktop devices, thus ensuring wider accessibility and ease of use. Features include:

  • Natural Language Processing: Utilizes advanced NLP techniques to understand and respond to user inquiries in natural language, enabling a seamless and intuitive user experience.
  • Dynamic FAQ: Dynamically generates responses to frequently asked questions, reducing the need for human intervention and ensuring consistency in the information provided.
  • Document Checklist: Provides users with a customized list of required documents based on the purpose of their appointment, ensuring that all necessary preparations are made in advance.

CoverUp: AI Cover Letter Writer

Tools: Java, HTML/CSS

Role: Software Developer, Creative Designer

CoverUp is an intelligent AI-powered application designed to streamline the job application process by generating personalized cover letters. Utilizing advanced natural language processing, CoverUp crafts high-quality cover letters based on the user’s resume, job description, company details, and any specific skills the user wishes to highlight. Key features of the app include:

  • Resume Integration: Seamlessly import resume details to ensure your cover letter accurately reflects your professional experience and qualifications.
  • Job Description Analysis: Tailor each cover letter to the specific job by analyzing the job description, ensuring your application stands out to potential employers.
  • Company Insights: Incorporate relevant company information to demonstrate your interest and knowledge about the organization.
  • Highlight Special Skills: Emphasize unique skills and achievements that set you apart from other candidates.
  • Cover Letter Storage: Store and access past cover letters for up to 60 days, allowing for easy retrieval and reuse for similar job applications.

Decomposure: 3D Video Game

Tools: Unity, C#, Blender

Role: Software Developer, Creative Designer

Decomposure is a unique apocalyptic organization game where players must navigate their survival within a confined, industrial room, relying on object-based text prompts to learn about their world. As apocalyptic events unfold, players face the dilemma of deciding which items to preserve and which to sacrifice. The game’s narrative is revealed through the exploration of the room and its objects, with large-scale disasters manifesting subtly through effects like window flashes, sounds, and camera shakes. This design choice emphasizes the gradual erosion of normalcy and highlights how trauma often accumulates through small, persistent disruptions rather than a single cataclysmic event. The game fosters a feeling of isolation and disconnect from the outside world, subverting the typical survival-focused narrative of apocalyptic games by centering around seemingly trivial personal items and their narratives.

SVD Image Compressor

Tools: Python, Numpy, Pillow

Role: Programmer

The SVD Image Compressor leverages Singular Value Decomposition to efficiently compress images, optimizing file sizes while maintaining high image quality. This tool can be beneficial for web developers, photographers, and anyone managing large digital image libraries because it offers significant reductions in storage. By providing an intuitive interface and adjustable compression levels, the compressor ensures that users can easily balance between quality and compression.

  • SVD-Based Compression: Decomposes images into singular values to efficiently reduce file size while preserving critical details.
  • Customizable Compression Levels: Allows users to choose the compression ratio, enabling a balance between image quality and file size.
  • Graphical Outputs: Generates graphs showing singular values, relative error, and rank of approximation, aiding in visual analysis of compression effectiveness.

Polaris: Safety App

Tools: Ruby, Java, Adobe CC

Role: Software Developer, Creative Designer

Polaris is a crowd-sourced crime tracking app designed to enhance personal safety and community awareness. Developed using Ruby and Java, Polaris provides real-time crime data and safety features to help users navigate their environments more securely. Key features of the app include:

  • Path Optimization: Leverage real-time crime data to suggest the safest routes, helping users avoid areas with ongoing or recent criminal activity.
  • Crime Filtering: Users can filter crime reports by type, such as theft, assault, or vandalism, to get detailed information relevant to their concerns.
  • User Reporting: Empower the community by allowing users to report crimes and suspicious activities directly through the app, contributing to a comprehensive crime database.
  • Emergency Contact Alerts: In case of emergencies, users can send alerts to their pre-set emergency contacts, including their current location information, ensuring timely assistance.
