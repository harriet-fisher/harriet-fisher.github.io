My Body Buddy

— Project Type

IOS Application

— Role

Developer and Creative Designer

— Date

2022

MyBodyBuddy is an interactive simulation app that acts as a satirical critique of diet culture. The app parodies popular diet apps and simulates social media experiences which enable and encourage unhealthy relationships with food and body image.

The App experience ends with a never-ending glitch that takes over the simulated device. This app is an art piece not intended for public distribution. For more information, please contact me at harrietfisher@uchicago.edu
